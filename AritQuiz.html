Arithmetic Quiz

A free HTML5 based application that auto-generates arithmetic quizzes consisting of multiple choice questions. No plugins needed. Use this to add Arithmetic Quizzes in publishing systems like Canvas, Brightspace, Blackboard, Moodle and WordPress.

Full Documentation: https://h5p.org/tutorial-arithmetic-quiz
